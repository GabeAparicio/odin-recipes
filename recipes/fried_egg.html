Fried Egg

Description:

Ahhh, a classic, the simple but unbeaten Fried Egg!!
Not much to say about this dish, it's delicious, nutritious, and incredibly easy to make!
If you need an explanation on a fried, then you need some help brother.


Ingredients:

  • Salt & pepper
  • Eggs (as many as you want)
  • Butter (unsalted)

Steps:

  1. Heat up the griddle. Heat the flat top griddle over medium low heat (approx. 325 degrees F).
    Melt the butter on the griddle and spread it out
  2. Begin cooking egg, crack the eggs onto the hot griddle. Use the tip of the griddle spatula to
    pull the whites further form the yolks in several spots. Let the eggs cook slowly for 4-6 minutes.
  3. Finish cooking egg, watch the white portions on the eggs closely and remove as soon as the white portion of the eggs are set.
  4. Serve and enjoy, remove the tasty fried eggs immediately from the head then they are ready to enjoy. Sprinkle salt & pepper
