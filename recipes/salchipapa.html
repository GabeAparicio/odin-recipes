Salchipapa

Description:

Salchipapa is a comfort food enjoyed across many countries in South America. As most comfort foods, the dish is actually quite simple,
it is made up of fried sausages and fries, that's it. While very simple to make, it is quite delicious to enjoy, usually eaten with mayo, ketchup,
and other sauces that you might enjoy. Let's jump into making this amazing dish!


Ingredients:

  • Salt & Pepper
  • 5 Medium potatoes
  • 1/2 Kg of sausages

Steps:

  1. Slice the potatoes into french fry size, place in hot oil that covers the potatoes fully, fry until crispy
  2. While the potatoes are frying, cut the sausages into 2 cm slices. Fry in another pan in some oil
  3. Season the fries as you wish, you could use some hot pepper flakes if you want an extra kick
  4. Combine the sausages and fries, top with sauces of your choice Enjoy!
