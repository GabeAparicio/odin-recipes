Ceviche

Description:

Ceviche is a delicious Peruvian fish dish that is very traditional and widely loved by its locals.
It consists of raw fish that is served with a ton of lime juice, which effectively cooks the fish due to its acidity.
Then it is mixed with onion and some spicy ingredient to add flavour.
It is usually accompanied by sweet potato, corn, toasted corn, and lettuce (as you can see in the picture).


Ingredients:

  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 Kg. fish of your choice
  • 1 Onion chopped into juliene slices
  • 1/4 Cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 Spicy yellow pepper chopped
  • 1 Aji limo chopped (decoration)
  • 12 Limes
  • 1 Pinch of salt & pepper
  • 1 Cup fish broth

To serve on the side:

  • 1 Tender corn boiled
  • 1 Sweet potato boiled
  • 1 Lettuce head

Steps:

  1. The first thing you must do to start this recipe is squeeze the 12 limes to extract its juice, save it for later. Then, cut the fish in 3 cm pieces, approximately.
    Place the pieces in the container which you will use to serve the dish
  2. Mix the fish with the chopped spicy yellow pepper, the broth and cilantro. Also place the chopped onion and crushed garlic.
    Let it rest for a few minutes to let it marinate well and allow time for the flavors to impregnate.
  3. Salt & pepper as you see fit and place on one side of the dish the sweet potato, lettuce, and corn.
  4. Finally, pour the lemon juice on the whole plate in a way that everything is well covered. Lastly, cut one slice of the aji limo rojo and place it as a garnish. Ready to eat!
